Concept

Hope's Abyss is a story-based game based on the Pandora's Box concept. It tells the dark tale of a young child, Jason, who finds a strange box in a forest. This box seems to be possessed by some sort of demonic soul, Despair, which Jason accidently releases. It is upto Jason and his only companion, a teddy named Hope to unravel Despairs games and lock her away back into the box.
